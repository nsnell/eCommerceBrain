# Customer Journey Intelligence

> **Load this file when the skill:** creates nurture sequences, onboarding flows, retention campaigns, win-back emails, upsell/cross-sell content, post-purchase flows, or any content that meets the customer at a specific stage of their relationship with the brand.
>
> **Do NOT load this file when the skill:** writes top-of-funnel brand awareness content, handles competitive positioning only, or performs style/voice tasks with no journey-stage context needed.

---

## How to Use This File

This file replaces the funnel diagram on a whiteboard. Instead of describing stages, it captures the actual mechanics: how people find you, what questions they ask during evaluation, what proof points close the sale, where they get stuck, and what triggers repeat purchases.

**To build yours:** Pull from analytics (actual conversion paths and drop-off points), sales/support call recordings (what objections close and what objections kill), post-purchase surveys, and customer interviews ("walk me through how you found us and decided to buy").
