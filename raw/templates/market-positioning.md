# Market Positioning Map

> **Load this file when the skill:** creates launch messaging, competitive comparisons, landing page copy, investor/partner decks, PR pitches, positioning statements, or any content that stakes a claim about what the brand is or how it's different.
>
> **Do NOT load this file when the skill:** writes routine content that doesn't involve differentiation claims (e.g., order confirmation emails, how-to blog posts, social engagement replies).

---

## How to Use This File

This file replaces the quarterly positioning slide deck. It's a living document that maps what territory you own, what's contested, and what you've ceded. Any skill that involves launch messaging, competitive differentiation, or content that stakes a claim reads this file.

**To build yours:** Audit competitor homepages (what are they actually claiming right now?), read G2/Trustpilot/Amazon reviews (how do customers compare you in their own words?), and review your win/loss data. Update monthly -- markets shift faster than quarterly decks.
