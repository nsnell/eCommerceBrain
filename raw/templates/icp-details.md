# ICP Details

> **Load this file when the skill:** writes any audience-facing content -- blog posts, social copy, emails, landing pages, ad copy, product descriptions, SMS campaigns, or any output that produces words a customer will read.
>
> **Do NOT load this file when the skill:** only needs competitive positioning, only needs the buyer's journey stages, or performs internal/operational tasks with no customer-facing output.

---

## How to Use This File

This file replaces a traditional ICP (Ideal Customer Profile). Instead of job titles and company sizes, it captures what makes your audience light up, what they complain about, and the actual words they use. Fill each section with real language pulled from reviews, Reddit threads, support tickets, social comments, and sales calls.

**To build yours:** Feed Claude your customer reviews, Reddit threads, support tickets, social comments, and competitor review sections. Ask it to extract patterns in their language, not your marketing department's language.
